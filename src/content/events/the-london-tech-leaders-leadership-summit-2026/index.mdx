[**London Tech Leaders**](https://www.londontechleaders.io/) (LTL) is **a premier, community-led events platform and networking group in the United Kingdom, designed specifically for senior technology executives, CTOs, and engineering leaders**. It serves as a space for industry veterans to share ideas, build strategic relationships, and tackle common industry challenges.

Diorama Consulting's founder, Mal, will be speaking at the event, alongside many other industry leaders and CTOs in London.
