Quantum computing suffers from an explanation problem: the analogies are everywhere
and the hands-on experience is nowhere. Quantum Curious closes that gap by letting
you build circuits and run them — first on a simulator for instant feedback, then
on real IBM quantum hardware via Qiskit.

A React frontend handles circuit building and visualisation; a FastAPI backend
brokers execution against IBM's quantum services.
