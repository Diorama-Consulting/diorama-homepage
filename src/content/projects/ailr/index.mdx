AILR is an interactive assessment tool that turns the fuzzy question "how ready are
we for AI?" into a structured, scored answer. A Next.js frontend walks you through
the assessment; a FastAPI backend scores responses and maintains your scorecard
across sessions.

It's a working example of how Diorama approaches readiness conversations with
clients: measure first, then prioritise.
