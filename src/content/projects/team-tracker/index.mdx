Team Tracker is the pragmatic sibling of Team Tapestry: where the Tapestry looks at
a team's history, the Tracker looks at its present. It keeps a simple, live picture
of who is on the team and what they're focused on — the kind of tool that usually
gets built as a spreadsheet and then outgrows one.
