The Mighty Fall is a passion project with a serious engineering core: an explorable,
annotated database covering the complete recorded output of The Fall. Song and album
data live in structured databases enriched with AI-assisted annotation, served
through a Python API.

It doubles as a demonstration of a pattern Diorama uses in client work — taking a
large, messy, deeply human dataset and making it navigable with a blend of
structured data and language models.
