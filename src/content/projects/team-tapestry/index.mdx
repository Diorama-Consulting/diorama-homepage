Team Tapestry visualises the one thing an org chart never shows: time. It renders a
team's history as an interactive tapestry, so growth phases, restructures and the
long arc of who-worked-with-whom become visible patterns.

There's a full write-up of the thinking and the build over on
[the insights section](/insights/team-tapestry).
