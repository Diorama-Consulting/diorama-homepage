AI Scout is the fully-automated successor to the AI News Agent: where the agent
organises the news for you to browse, the Scout reads it for you and reports back.
Every morning it surveys curated sources, synthesises the developments that matter
using several frontier language models, generates an accompanying infographic, and
delivers the finished briefing by email.

It has produced a briefing every single day since deployment — a working example of
the kind of unattended, multi-model agent pipeline that features in Diorama's
advisory work.
