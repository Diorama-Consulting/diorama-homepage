A typical engagement consists of four stages:

1. **Discovery**: to clarify goals, constraints, metrics.
2. **Proposal**: to scope outcomes, cadence, governance.
3. **Engagement**: to build, coach, deliver and evaluate.
4. **Measure**: to track ROI/OKRs, adjust and handover.
